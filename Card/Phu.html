The four-year journey at CSTU begins with self-discovery and skill-building, followed by focusing on specialized learning and goal setting. In the third year, students gain real-world experience through internships or projects. By the fourth year, they are ready to step into their careers or further studies, equipped with the skills and confidence to succeed.
